CHRD MOBILE APPLICATION: COLLABORATED

Project Purpose: Chrd is a mobile platform designed to connect live music venues, musicians and fans. This app addresses a persistent challenge in the live entertainment industry: venues and bands struggle to efficiently find, communicate and book each other; attract audiences; and fans unable to locate a music venue or band without extensive effort. By utilizing a unique user-friendly platform that includes AI matchmaking, location-based services, and a secure payment system, Chrd provides an all-in-one solution for discovering, booking, and promoting live music. The concept of seamlessly connecting venues, musicians and fans together with a music friendly vibe yet powerful platform is referred to as the Chrd Triangle
